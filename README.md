# Extended Essay
Extended essay for EC424 Monetary Economics and Aggregate Fluctuation at LSE, 2025/26.

## Topic
The 2022 Energy Crisis resulted in a spike in oil prices and subsequent inflation in the UK and other countries.
While the surge in oil prices was relatively temporary and went back to the previous level relatively quickly, the UK economy has experienced persistent inflation.
This inflation persistency cannot be observed using a standard New Keynesian (NK) model for a small open economy that imports oil at an exogenous price for home production.
The aim of this extended essay project is to extend the NK model and examine whether an inflation persistency can be generated in figuring out the causes of what the real economy has observed since 2022.
